Let's Play Rock Paper Scissor

Please select the player options.

Single Player Rules

  • You will be playing against the computer.

  • You can choose between Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

  • The first one to three points wins.

  • Here are the rules to getting a point in the game:


  • Rock beats Scissors
    Scissors beats Paper
    Paper beats Rock


  • If the player and computer choose the same option (Ex. Paper and Paper), then no one gets the point.

Multi Player Rules

  • You will be playing against another player.

  • You and another player can choose between Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

  • The first one to three points wins.

  • When first player chosing it's decision,
    then second player don't look at the chosen decision by the first player.

  • Here are the rules to getting a point in the game:

  • Rock beats Scissors
    Scissors beats Paper
    Paper beats Rock


  • If the player 1 and player 2 choose the same option (Ex. Paper and Paper), then no one gets the point.
